Steps to Rubber Duck Debugging

  1. Get a rubber duck
  2. Place your rubber duck where you are working on code
  3. When stuck or unsure of what you are doing, tell your rubber duck about it
  4. Be detailed and exact in your discription
  5. Find and fix the bugs and errors together
